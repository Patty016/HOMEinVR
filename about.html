Project Overview

Hall of Mental-Health Experiences
HOMEinVR

HOME is a virtual reality museum experience that explores five different mental illnesses: OCD, PTSD, DPDR, Panic Disorder, and Schizophrenia. HOME seeks to lessen the stigmas of mental illness by providing a simulated experience of them. Venture into these immersive experiences as they represent symptoms that people with these illness deal with on a daily basis.

Meet the Team

Hello and welcome to HOME (Hall of Mental Health Experiences). We are fourth year students in the Interactive Arts and Science program at Brock University. Our team consists of awesome creative people who have worked super hard to bring this experience to life.
